About me

I am a digital artist from Mexico, who loves to design and create original characters, as well as create content of my favorite characters from any series.

I try to be inspired by cartoonish, semi-realistic and anime / manga drawing styles.

My main drawing software are Krita from PC and IbisPaintX from cell phone, likewise, I have experience with platforms such as Clip Studio Paint and Medibang Paint.
